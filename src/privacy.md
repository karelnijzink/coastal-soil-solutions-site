# Privacy & consent

**Plain language, because that is how we do everything.**

## What this site collects

Nothing beyond what you send us. There is no contact form, no newsletter signup and no advertising tracker on this site. If we add simple visitor counting later, it will be a privacy-respecting counter that uses no cookies and identifies no one, and this page will say so.

## What happens when you email or call

Your message, name and contact details are used to reply to you and to do the work you asked about. We do not sell or share them. If you want anything we hold deleted, email <tomlinsonbc@gmail.com> and it will be done.

## Commercial email and CASL

If we ever send commercial email, it will follow Canada's Anti-Spam Legislation: we will identify ourselves, include a working unsubscribe, and send only where we have consent or a genuine business relationship. You will never be added to a list by contacting us with a question.
